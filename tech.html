Huawei MateBook: The iPad Pro Rival Coming Soon to India

Huawei used its P9 launch event to make a surprise announcement about its newest tablet - the Huawei MateBook - which will soon make its entry into the Indian market. The convertible tablet was first introduced at the Mobile World Congress in February this year. Aimed at the premium business market, the Huawei MateBook will compete with Apple's iPad Pro and the Microsoft Surface.

Hacking Group Claims to Auction Spy Tools Linked to the NSA

Hackers going by the name Shadow Brokers said on Monday they will auction stolen surveillance tools they say were used by a cyber group linked to the US National Security Agency. To arouse interest in the auction, the hackers released samples of programs they said could break into popular firewall software made by companies including Cisco Systems Inc, Juniper Networks Inc and Fortinet Inc.

NASA Mulling Russia's Proposal to Cut Down Crew at Space Station

NASA is weighing a Moscow proposal to cut the number of Russian cosmonauts at the International Space Station from three to two, particularly its potential risk to the crew, an official said Monday. Typically, six crew members live at the orbiting outpost, a hallmark of global cooperation that is meant to stay in operation until at least 2024.
